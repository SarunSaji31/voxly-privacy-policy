Privacy Policy — Voxly AI Keyboard

Effective date: 17 March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Voxly AI Keyboard Android application (the "Application"), developed by Sarun Saji ("Service Provider"). By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. What the Application Does

Voxly AI Keyboard is an Android input method (keyboard) that allows users to type using their voice. The Application records audio from your microphone, sends it to an AI service for transcription and translation, and inputs the resulting text into any app on your device. Users can also create a cloned voice profile for text-to-speech playback.

2. Information We Collect

Audio recordings
When you tap the microphone button, the Application records audio from your device microphone. This audio is transmitted to Google Gemini AI for transcription and translation. Audio files are processed in real time and are not permanently stored by the Service Provider.

Google account information
The Application uses Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication). When you sign in, we collect your Google account email address and a unique user identifier (UID). We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google data.

Voice profile data
If you choose to create a voice clone, your recorded voice sample is sent to ElevenLabs for voice cloning. This data is stored on ElevenLabs servers and governed by ElevenLabs own privacy policy (elevenlabs.io/privacy).

Credit balance and usage data
We store your credit balance and usage history in Google Firestore to manage your account and provide the service.

Device information
We may collect basic device information such as your IP address, Android version, and device model for error monitoring and service improvement.

3. Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Gemini AI — audio transcription and translation (ai.google.dev)
  • ElevenLabs — voice cloning and text-to-speech (elevenlabs.io/privacy)

Audio sent to Google Gemini and ElevenLabs is subject to their respective data processing terms. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide voice transcription and translation features
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To generate and play back cloned voice audio (if you create a voice profile)
  • To improve the Application and fix errors
  • To communicate important updates about the service

5. Microphone Permission

The Application requires microphone access solely for voice input. The microphone is only active when you explicitly tap the microphone button in the keyboard. The Application does not record audio in the background.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Audio recordings are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and are not permanently stored on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data (email, credit balance, usage history) for as long as your account is active. Voice profiles stored on ElevenLabs are retained until you delete them from within the Application. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at sarunsaji31@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Opt-Out and Account Deletion

You can stop using the Application at any time by uninstalling it. To request deletion of your account and personal data, email sarunsaji31@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account".
