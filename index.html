Privacy Policy — Voxly AI Keyboard

Effective date: 17 March 2026  ·  Developer: Sarun Saji

This Privacy Policy describes how Voxly AI Keyboard ("Application", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Android keyboard application. By using the Application you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address via Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). We use this only to identify your account and manage your credit balance.

Voice and audio data. The Application records audio from your microphone when you tap the mic button. This audio is transmitted to our backend server and then to Google Gemini AI solely to perform speech transcription and translation. Audio files are deleted immediately after processing and are never stored permanently.

Voice clone samples. If you choose to use the voice cloning feature, you may upload a voice recording. This recording is sent to ElevenLabs (ElevenLabs Inc.) to create a voice clone. Your voice data is processed under ElevenLabs' own privacy policy. You can delete your voice profile at any time from within the Application.

Usage and credit data. We store your credit balance, usage history, and account settings in Firebase Firestore (Google LLC). This is necessary to provide the credit-based service.

Device and technical data. We may collect your device's IP address, operating system version, and crash logs to maintain and improve the Application.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe and translate your speech into text
  • To generate voice audio using your cloned voice
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing
  • To improve the Application's performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google LLC) — user login and data storage
  • Google Gemini AI (Google LLC) — speech transcription and translation
  • ElevenLabs (ElevenLabs Inc.) — voice cloning and text-to-speech generation
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC) — in-app credit purchases

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Microphone Permission

The Application requires microphone access solely to capture your voice for transcription. The microphone is only active when you explicitly tap the mic button. We do not record audio in the background.

5. In-App Purchases

The Application offers credit packages purchasable through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

6. Data Retention

Your account data (email, credit balance) is retained for as long as your account is active. Voice recordings used for transcription are deleted immediately after processing. Voice clone samples stored on ElevenLabs may be deleted by you at any time via the Settings screen. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly. Users must be at least 16 years old to consent to data processing in most countries.

8. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission between the Application and our servers. Your account data is stored in Google Firebase which provides industry-standard security. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can:

  • Delete your voice profiles from the Settings screen
  • Sign out of your account at any time
  • Request full account and data deletion by contacting us
  • Uninstall the Application to stop all data collection

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
