Privacy Policy — Voxly AI Keyboard

Effective date: 10 June 2026  ·  Developer: Sarun Saji

This Privacy Policy describes how Voxly AI Keyboard ("Application", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Android keyboard application. By using the Application you agree to the practices described here.

1. Keyboard and Typing Data

Your typing stays on your device. Voxly is an input method (keyboard) app, so we want to be very clear: the text you type with the keyboard is processed only on your device and is delivered directly to the app you are typing into. We do not log, store, or transmit your keystrokes.
  • Word suggestions and autocorrect are generated entirely on your device using a dictionary bundled inside the Application. No typed text leaves your device for these features.
  • Suggestions and autocorrect are automatically disabled in password, email address, and web address fields.
  • We do not collect data about which apps you type into.

The only content that leaves your device is content you explicitly send: voice recordings you make by tapping the mic button (for transcription) and voice samples you choose to upload (for voice cloning), as described below.

2. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address via Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). We use this only to identify your account and manage your credit balance.

Voice and audio data (transcription). The Application records audio from your microphone only when you tap the mic button. This audio is transmitted over an encrypted connection to our backend server (hosted in the European Union) and then to Google Gemini AI solely to perform speech transcription and translation. Audio files are deleted from our server immediately after processing and are never stored permanently. If the recording is silent or empty, it is rejected on your device and is not transmitted at all.

Voice clone samples. If you choose to use the voice cloning feature, you may record and upload a voice sample. This recording is sent to ElevenLabs (ElevenLabs Inc.) to create a voice clone, processed under ElevenLabs' own privacy policy. To operate this feature, our server also stores a reference linking your profile to the ElevenLabs voice and a non-reversible content fingerprint (hash) of your sample used to avoid duplicate clones. Voice tuning preferences (such as speed and stability) are stored only on your device. You can delete your voice profiles at any time from the Application's Settings or Manage Voices screen; deletion removes the clone from ElevenLabs and the associated data from our server.

Generated voice clips. Audio clips generated with your cloned voice are saved in the Application's private storage on your device. A clip is shared with another app (for example WhatsApp) only when you explicitly choose to share it via the Android share sheet, and only the app you select receives access to that clip.

Account and credit data. We store your credit balance and account status in Firebase Firestore (Google LLC). This is necessary to provide the credit-based service.

Device and technical data. Through Firebase, we may collect your device's IP address, operating system version, basic app usage events, and crash logs to maintain and improve the Application.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe and translate your speech into text
  • To create voice clones and generate voice audio, when you use those features
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To improve the Application's performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google LLC) — user login and account data storage
  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — anonymous usage statistics and crash reporting
  • Google Gemini AI (Google LLC) — speech transcription and translation
  • ElevenLabs (ElevenLabs Inc.) — voice cloning and text-to-speech generation (only if you use the voice cloning feature)
  • Google Play (Google LLC) — app distribution and in-app update checks

5. Microphone Permission

The Application requires microphone access solely to capture your voice for transcription and, if you choose, voice clone samples. The microphone is only active when you explicitly tap the mic or record button, and the keyboard shows when recording is in progress. We do not record audio in the background.

6. Credits

The Application uses a credit system to meter transcription and voice generation. New accounts receive free credits, and credits are only deducted for successful requests. The Application does not currently offer in-app purchases. If paid credit packages are introduced in the future, payments will be handled by Google Play and this policy will be updated before launch.

7. Data Retention

  • Typed text: never collected, so there is nothing to retain.
  • Transcription audio: deleted from our server immediately after processing.
  • Voice clones and sample fingerprints: retained until you delete the voice profile in the Application, or until your account is deleted.
  • Generated voice clips: stored only on your device; removed when you clear the Application's data or uninstall it.
  • Account data (email, credit balance): retained for as long as your account is active.

To request full account deletion, visit our Account Deletion page or contact us at the email below.

8. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

To request account deletion, visit our Account Deletion page or email us directly at Sarunsaji31@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 business days.

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your credit balance
  • Your voice clones on ElevenLabs, and the voice references and sample fingerprints on our server
  • All personal data associated with your account

9. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. In regions where the law sets a higher minimum age for consenting to data processing (up to 16 in parts of the European Union), you must meet that age or have parental consent to use the Application. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Data Security

All data transmission between the Application, our servers, and third-party services uses HTTPS encryption. Every request to our backend is authenticated with your Firebase session token. Your account data is stored in Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard security. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location (including under the GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. You can:

  • Delete individual voice profiles from the Settings or Manage Voices screen
  • Sign out of your account at any time
  • Request full account and data deletion via the Account Deletion page
  • Uninstall the Application to stop all data collection
  • Email us to exercise any other data right

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example when new features are added. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
