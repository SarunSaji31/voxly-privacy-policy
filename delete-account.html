Delete Your Voxly Account

You can request deletion of your Voxly AI Keyboard account and all associated data at any time. This process is free and will be completed within 7 business days.

Request account deletion by emailing:

Sarunsaji31@gmail.com

Use subject line: Account Deletion Request
Include your registered Google account email in the message.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and Google Sign-In credentials
  • Your credit balance and purchase history
  • Your voice profiles and recordings
  • All personal data stored in our systems
  • Your voice clone on ElevenLabs servers

What happens after deletion

  • Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • You will no longer be able to sign in to Voxly
  • Any unused credits will be forfeited
  • This action cannot be undone
Note: You can also delete your voice profiles directly from the Voxly app Settings screen without contacting us.
